## First

I finally built my personal blog site.

There are mainly two reasons why I wanted to create this site.

First, having a place like this gives me a space to share my thoughts and outputs. Writing articles helps me understand what I’ve learned more deeply.

Second, I wanted to have something of my own on the web — a small space that feels like my own place.

## What’s next

I’ll write articles about things I learn and topics I’m interested in.  
I also want to add more features to this website over time.

I know there are still many things I could improve, but “done is better than perfect.”  
So I decided to publish it first instead of trying to make everything perfect from the beginning.
